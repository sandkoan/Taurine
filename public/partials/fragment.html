Welcome to Taurine! Whether you're directing an annual housewrite, assembling your team's ACF packet, or just compiling sets for personal use, Taurine is the all-purpose organizational tool that you may or may not have always wanted. Just like NAQT's proprietary Ginseng software, Taurine is also named after an ingredient on the back of my energy drinks.

Disclaimer: I am a rising college freshman who is hoping to try his hand at college bowl. The practical implication of this fact is that there exists a possibility I might play on any college sets written on this server; while I promise to disclose any inadvertent exposure of your questions, you might prefer to host a copy of Taurine on your own domain. Please contact me if that is the case. I totally understand in light of the recent security scandals.
Special thanks to the Raleigh Charter High School QB Team for their tireless usage and constructive criticism of Taurine's predecessor.

Thanks also to Charles Hang of Olympia Questions for an extensive list of suggestions. Many of these improvements are reflected in Taurine.

Taurine is free for personal and commercial use of any kind. If you're unsure whether a particular use case is appropriate, please consult the extremely concise and clear license agreement.
Please log in using the following form. Multiple usernames can be associated with the same email; therefore, you must use your username rather than your email address. If you have forgotten any of your credentials, please pay a visit to the Forgot page.
Please register using the following form. I encourage the usage of real names for all work associated with a given individual; this optional measure will decrease any potential confusion about your identity in the future. The server does enforce certain validation criteria for your credentials:

  • Username: Must be between three and twenty alphanumeric characters.
  • Email: Must be a valid email between six and two hundred characters.
  • Password: Must be eight to forty characters of any type.
  • Name: Must be one to sixty characters of any type.
This form allows you to recover your login credentials. If you forgot your password, enter your username and email to receive a temporary password. If you forgot your username, enter only an email to receive a list of usernames associated with the given address. If you forgot your email, I don't know what to say.
